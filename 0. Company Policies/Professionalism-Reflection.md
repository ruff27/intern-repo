What are examples of professional vs. unprofessional behaviour in a casual work environment? (Hint: Think about communication, meeting etiquette, and teamwork.)
Professional behaviour includes arriving on time, being responsive and polite in conversations, acknowledging others' opinions and contributions, listening actively.
Unprofessional behaviour includes missing deadlines, interrupting teammates for no reason, sharing confidential information carelessly.

What does respectful communication look like in remote teams? (e.g., tone in messages, handling disagreements, responding to feedback)
Use clear and friendly tone in messages
Avoid using harsh punctuation
Be kind and specific when giving feedback

How can you give and receive feedback professionally? (e.g., focus on actions, not people; assume good intent; ask for clarification instead of reacting defensively)
Focus on behaviours and outcomes, not the person
Use words such was "I noticed" or "I suggest"
Listen fully before responding
Highlight positivies before areas to improve while giving feedback

Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?
Yes. My team leader ensures everyone is felt safe to share ideas and ask questions and encouraged open discussions, use light humour to build connections, and made ensure everyone felt included.

What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?
Use concise messages and confirm understanding
Check the tone before sending any message - ensuring no one feels offended
Be mindful of neurodiversity and cultural differences when phrasing messages.

How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?
Celebrate small wins in discord channels
Recognise others' works publicly, give feedback privately
Fill in to support when workloads get heavy

How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?
Review agenda or project notes prior to meetings
Bring in 1-2 talking points or updates
Take notes from meetings to follow-up

What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?
Regularly share progress
Volunteer for small tasks or improvements
Offer solutions when raising a problem

How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?
Send a follow-up message if no response has been made in 2-3 days
Email or tag the relevant colleague on discord if the issue is still unresolved

What are some things you won't do?
Gossip or talk negatively of teammates
Ignore feedback
Share confidential information
Use tone that alienate others
