What are common cyber security threats in a remote work environment?
Phishing emails, weak or reusing passwords, malware, and device theft.

What are best practices for keeping your devices and accounts secure?
Using Two-Factor Authentication as well as using a password manager. Lock your device when not in use.

Why is it important to lock your computer when away from your desk?
Confidential information might get leaked and be abused.

How should you handle phishing attempts and suspicious links?
Verify the email address or the type of message sent.

What makes a strong password, and why should you use a password manager?
A strong password consists of using symbols, uppercase and lowercase characters, and numbers and have a long passoword. Password managers eliminate the need to remember all of their passwords.

Why is two-factor authentication (2FA) important, and when should you enable it?
2FA is an extra layer of authentication asking to type in a passcode sent to email/SMS/authenticator app. It should be used when logging into financial accounts, social media, and work accounts.
